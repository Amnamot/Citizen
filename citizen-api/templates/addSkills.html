This section assigns

skills

and competencies that allow a person to effectively perform various tasks and achieve their goals. Skills can be either general or specialized, and can relate to different areas such as communication, leadership, creative thinking, time management, and many others.

For example, generic skills may be communication, leadership, presentation, and time management skills that are important to work in any field of endeavor. Specialized skills may relate to a specific profession or field, such as programming, design, marketing, etc.

Skill development is an important element of personal and professional development, and can occur through formal education and training as well as through self-study and practice. A combination of general and specialized skills can help a person do their job effectively, achieve their goals, and improve their skill level.
