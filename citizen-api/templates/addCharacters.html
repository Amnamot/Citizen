Character

is a stable and individual set of qualities that determines how a person interacts with the world around him or her, his or her preferences, inclinations, motivations, behavioral and emotional reactions.

Some of the qualities may be more pronounced than others and may manifest differently depending on the situation and context. In addition, character can develop and change over the course of life as a result of experience, learning, and interaction with the world around us.

Character traits subconsciously influence one decision or another, without much consciousness. And only the subsequent evaluation of the consequences of the action and conclusions, lead to changes in the character and behavior of the person - so there is personal growth.
